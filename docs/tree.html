Decision tree — how triggers become tickers

How the solution prunes the implication network down to tickers:
  1. 📣 Trigger → chain. Each high-reach post is laddered into a causal chain — the hops shown left-to-right (parsed from the curator's mechanism).
  2. Middle-band filter. Keep chains of depth 2–3 (deep enough the herd hasn't priced them, past the obvious hop-1, short of hop-4+ storytelling). Red trigger = KEPT (the bet); faded = pruned.
  3. 🎯 Vehicle selection. At the chain's end, pick the purest instrument — the rate/commodity ETN over a diluted operator equity (this is what surfaces BWET).
  4. Mechanical sizing downstream (optimizer; the LLM never sets weights).
Caveat: only the chain the curator committed to is recorded — the alternative branches it weighed and rejected are not stored, so this shows the chosen path, not every pruned branch.
