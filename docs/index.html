Through the herd, on $50K

Hindsight upper bound, not forward lift. This is the event-first agent finding BWET in a realistic, noisy GDELT news firehose — with BWET's early under-the-radar article seeded (real search misses those niche pieces). It still leans on that seed and on a model trained past the events, so it does not prove the firehose finds gems in time. The clean verdict is the forward eval (src/forward.py).

The firehose portfolio: each week, the gems the financial press names as thesis-driven movers go on the watchlist; a position is held while its driving thesis is live and dropped when it decays. A plain mean-variance optimizer sizes it — the LLM never sets a weight.
